Academic excellence is no longer the only criteria for job surety. One must be trained to effectively present such knowledge. IIMBx in an effort to blend industrial skills with OVERVIEW theoretical learning has introduced Academic Partnership Programmes.

Academic institutes can partner with IIMBx to enhance their campus offerings through online programmes designed to integrate with academic curriculum.

Students at undergraduate and graduate levels have an opportunity to gain business and managerial skills along with their conventional academic endeavor. On completion of such programmes, students will receive certificates from IIMBx.

Academic Partnership programmes from IIMBx are offered in two formats.

  • Online certificate programme in Fundamentals of Business Management
  • Specialized Management Courses in a wide range of disciplines
